# Code of Conduct

1321 adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant]. This policy applies to project
spaces and to anyone representing the project in public.

## Expected conduct

- Treat other people with respect.
- Give specific, constructive feedback.
- Accept correction without hostility.
- Protect private information and unpublished reports.
- Put the health of the project and its community ahead of personal conflict.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, and sustained
personal attacks are not accepted. Publishing another person's private information without
permission is not accepted.

## Report a conduct issue

Email [hello@athledev.com](mailto:hello@athledev.com). Do not open a public issue for a private
conduct report.

Include the relevant links, dates, participants, and enough context to assess what happened. Share
only information you are authorized to disclose. Reports will be handled as privately as practical.

If the report concerns the repository owner, use [GitHub's abuse reporting channel][github-abuse]
instead of sending the report to the project address.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove content, restrict participation, or ban a participant when needed to
protect the project and its community. Enforcement should be proportionate, documented privately,
and consistent with the Contributor Covenant enforcement guidelines.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
[github-abuse]: https://support.github.com/contact/report-abuse
